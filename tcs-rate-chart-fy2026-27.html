TCS · Section 206C

TCS Rate Chart FY 2026-27

Tax collected at source (TCS) under Section 206C applies to specified sales and remittances at rates from 0.1% to 20%. Common cases include scrap (1%), motor vehicles above Rs.10 lakh (1%), and Liberalised Remittance Scheme remittances. Thresholds and rates change — confirm before applying.

Frequently asked questions

What is the TCS rate on a motor vehicle?

1% under Section 206C(1F) on the sale of a motor vehicle where the value exceeds Rs.10 lakh.

What is the TCS on foreign remittance under LRS?

TCS under Section 206C(1G) applies above the notified threshold, at 5% or 20% depending on the purpose (education, medical or other). Confirm the current threshold and rate.

Does TCS on sale of goods (206C(1H)) still apply?

The 206C(1H) levy on sale of goods above Rs.50 lakh has been the subject of change; verify whether it is in force for your period.
