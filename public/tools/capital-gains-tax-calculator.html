Property Details

Enter property details on the left to compute capital gains.

Section 50C — Stamp Duty Value (SDV) rule: If the sale consideration is lower than the SDV (circle rate / guidance value), the SDV is deemed to be the sale consideration for computing capital gains, unless the difference is ≤10% of the actual consideration. If the taxpayer disputes the SDV, a reference can be made to the Valuation Officer under Section 50C(2).
Finance (No. 2) Act, 2024 — option for pre-23 July 2024 property: For transfers on or after 23 July 2024 of property acquired before that date, taxpayers can compare (a) 12.5% without indexation vs (b) 20% with CII indexation, and choose whichever is lower. This calculator computes both and highlights the better option.

Equity / Listed Share Details

Enter share details on the left to compute capital gains tax.

Grandfathering (Section 112A proviso): For listed equity shares/units acquired before 31 January 2018, the cost of acquisition is deemed to be the higher of: (a) actual cost, or (b) lower of FMV as on 31 Jan 2018 and actual sale consideration. Enter the FMV per share in the field above if applicable.

Mutual Fund Details

Enter MF details on the left to compute capital gains tax.

Debt MF — Finance Act 2023 amendment (w.e.f. 1 April 2023): Gains on debt MF units purchased on or after 1 April 2023 are taxable as short-term capital gains at slab rates, regardless of holding period. Indexation benefit is not available on such units. Units purchased before 1 April 2023 retain grandfathered LTCG treatment at 20% with indexation if held >36 months.
