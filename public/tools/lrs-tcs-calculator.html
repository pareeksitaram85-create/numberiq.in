Finance Act 2025 change: The threshold for LRS TCS was raised from ₹7 lakh to ₹10 lakh per financial year w.e.f. 1 April 2025. The rate structure (Nil below threshold; 0.5%/5%/20% above) remains otherwise unchanged from the Finance (No. 2) Act, 2024 amendments.
No PAN (Section 206CC): If the remitter does not furnish PAN/Aadhaar, TCS is collected at twice the applicable rate or 5%, whichever is higher. For 20% categories this becomes 40%; for 5% categories this becomes 10%; for 0.5% it becomes 5%.
