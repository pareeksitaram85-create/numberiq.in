Frequently asked questions

How many GST slabs are there?

The main slabs are 0%, 5%, 12%, 18% and 28%, with special rates for items like gold (3%) and rough diamonds (0.25%), plus a compensation cess on certain luxury and sin goods.

Who decides GST rates?

The GST Council, a constitutional body of the Centre and States, decides and revises GST rates; changes are notified by the CBIC.

How do I find the GST rate for a specific item?

Identify its HSN (goods) or SAC (services) code and check the notified rate. Use the NumberIQ HSN/SAC Finder and confirm on cbic.gov.in.
