What is GSTR-9?

SRNumberIQ GlossaryLast reviewed June 2026

GSTR-9 is the annual GST return that consolidates a financial year's outward and inward supplies, ITC and tax into one return per GSTIN.

How it works

It is mandatory for taxpayers with aggregate turnover above Rs.2 crore and is due by 31 December following the financial year. It cannot be revised after filing.

Reference. Section 44, CGST Act; Rule 80.

Frequently asked questions

Who must file GSTR-9?

Registered persons with aggregate annual turnover above Rs.2 crore; below that it is optional.

When is GSTR-9 due?

By 31 December following the end of the financial year.

Key takeaways

  • GSTR-9 is the GST annual return.
  • Mandatory above Rs.2 crore turnover.
  • Due 31 December; cannot be revised.
